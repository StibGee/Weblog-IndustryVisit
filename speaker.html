Meet Our Speakers

Join us for an inspiring industry visit featuring expert speakers at the forefront of technology and innovation. Discover firsthand insights from leaders who are shaping the future of computer science. Gain valuable perspectives, expand your knowledge, and ignite your passion for the limitless possibilities in technology.

Dave Del Rosario

An FAA staff member introduced their virtual flight training software, which provides students with realistic aviation scenarios, eliminating the need for actual aircraft. This tool enhances learning by simulating real-world flight conditions.

Engr. Adrian Salda

At LRT2, Sir Adrian briefed on train operations, safety, and showcased their tracking software. He also touched on the authority's commitment to maintaining rail integrity and enhancing passenger experience.

Karla Legaspi

At QBO Innovation Hub, she outlined their company's focus on startups and their commitment to supporting recent graduates, providing them with a nurturing environment to launch their careers.

Rose Anne Caraan

At PACKETWORX, she detailed their company's focus on developing advanced IoT devices, connecting multiple devices for enhanced functionality. She showcased their innovative products and explained how their hardware collects data, which is then securely transmitted to their online dashboard for comprehensive analysis and future research, highlighting the practical benefits of their cutting-edge technology.

Arnel Gonzales

At the PAGASA Weather Station in Subic, he explained the precise operations behind weather forecasting, utilizing sophisticated software and hardware to analyze wind directions and other meteorological data. This expertise enables accurate predictions of weather patterns for upcoming days, weeks, and months.

Engr. Jerome Saddi

At the Vessel Traffic Management in Subic, an engineer demonstrated the techniques for tracking incoming and outgoing ships, measuring distances, and verifying their boundaries. This critical tracking ensures ship safety and security, aiding in precise arrival time predictions, which is vital for optimizing maritime logistics and planning.

Brian Tan Seng

Sir Brian Tan Seng, CEO of 98Labs Incorporation, leads a company renowned for crafting bespoke websites and software solutions for a diverse clientele. With a portfolio of successful products across various industries, 98Labs specializes in software development, distinguishing itself through its innovative approach and tailored technological offerings.

Gretchel Listana

On a tour with Gretchel from Hytec Power Inc., I explored their advanced facilities, learning about their innovative power solutions and renewable technologies. Her expertise and passion for sustainability made the experience both informative and inspiring.
